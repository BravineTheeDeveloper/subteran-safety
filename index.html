Pest Control

  • Domestic Fumigation

    We offer expert home fumigation services to eradicate pests like cockroaches, termites, and rodents. Using safe and effective treatments, we create a healthy, pest-free environment for your household. Our customized solutions ensure long-lasting protection while prioritizing the safety of your family and pets.

  • Commercial Fumigation

    Our commercial fumigation services protect businesses, warehouses, and industries from pests that can damage goods and disrupt operations. We use industry-approved treatments to eliminate infestations while ensuring compliance with hygiene regulations. Our long-term pest management plans guarantee a safe and pest-free business environment.

  • Bedbug Extermination

    Bedbugs cause discomfort and health risks if left untreated, making effective extermination essential. Our specialized treatments include heat, chemical, and other advanced solutions to eliminate bedbugs at all life stages. We thoroughly remove infestations in homes, hotels, and commercial properties with guaranteed results.

  • Ship Fumigation

    Ships and cargo containers are prone to pest infestations, leading to contamination and compliance issues. Our ship fumigation services target insects, rodents, and other pests using specialized treatments. We ensure vessels meet international standards while protecting cargo integrity and minimizing downtime.

  • Agribusiness Fumigation

    Protecting stored grains, crops, and farm facilities from pests is vital for food safety and productivity. Our agribusiness fumigation services offer eco-friendly and effective pest control solutions for silos, warehouses, and agricultural spaces. We ensure compliance with safety standards while preventing contamination and losses.

  • Post & Preconstruction Fumigation

    Construction sites are vulnerable to termites and pests that cause structural damage. Our pre-construction fumigation creates a barrier to prevent future infestations, while post-construction treatments eliminate existing threats. We help ensure long-term durability and compliance with safety standards.

  • Large Sale Humidification

    Controlling humidity is essential for preventing pest infestations and product deterioration in large facilities. Our large-scale humidification services regulate moisture levels in warehouses, factories, and greenhouses. We use advanced humidity control systems to improve air quality, prevent mold, and maintain stored goods.

  • Mould Removal

    Excess moisture creates conditions for harmful mould growth, which threatens health and weakens structures. Our professional mould removal services detect, treat, and prevent mould infestations using industry-approved solutions. We restore indoor air quality, protect surfaces, and ensure a safe living or working environment.
